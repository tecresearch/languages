About us

"Nagar Nigam," also known as a Municipal Corporation, is a local governing body responsible for the administration and management of a city or urban area in India. The primary functions of a Nagar Nigam include maintaining public infrastructure, ensuring cleanliness and sanitation, managing waste disposal, providing essential services like water supply and street lighting, maintaining parks and public spaces, and implementing urban planning and development projects.
